The Signature Selection

Curated Liquid Craftsmanship

Each beverage in our signature line is developed as a premium product launch. We combine strict extraction principles with culinary innovation.

Signature Speciality Beverage

Crème Brûlée Iced Latte

Torched Demerara Double Espresso Vanilla Pastry Crème

Our hallmark dessert-espresso fusion. We pull a double shot of our house espresso, combined with chilled textured milk over ice, and top it with a thick layer of vanilla pastry cream, finished by caramelizing raw Demerara sugar to a crackling, golden-brown crust. Break the top with a spoon before your first sip.

The Craft Behind the Brew

Torched immediately before serving to achieve a glass-like caramelized shell that offers a crispy contrast to the smooth, rich iced coffee below.

₹280
Iced Speciality Tea

Caramel Matcha

Ceremonial Uji Matcha House Caramel Organic Milk

A double-layered presentation of sweet and earthy. Whisked ceremonial Uji Matcha floated over chilled, organic whole milk and a rich layer of house-made salted caramel syrup. A beautiful graphic contrast with a balanced, lingering sweet finish.

The Craft Behind the Brew

Whisked at exactly 80°C to activate the matcha flavor profile without bitterness, creating a beautiful graphic contrast when poured over chilled milk layers.

₹300
Gourmet Culinary Signature

Cottage Cheese Rice Bowl

Grilled Paneer Herbed Rice Creamy Makhani Sauce

A luxurious fusion bowl. Fresh, succulent grilled cottage cheese steak layered over fragrant herbed rice, served in a rich, velvety spiced tomato crème sauce. Finished with organic microgreens and a drizzle of herb-infused chili oil.

The Craft Behind the Dish

Balanced at precisely 72°C to preserve the softness of the cottage cheese while ensuring the makhani sauce achieves its signature glossy emulsion.

₹350
Artisanal Editorial Dessert

Mango Sticky Rice

Alphonso Mango Steamed Sticky Rice Coconut Crème Reduction

A classic Thai delicacy elevated to a fine dining art. High-grade glutinous sweet rice steamed in pandan leaves, accompanied by sweet, hand-sliced seasonal Alphonso mangoes. Served over a rich, salted warm coconut crème reduction and toasted sesame seeds.

The Craft Behind the Dessert

The sticky rice is soaked for exactly 12 hours and steamed, while the coconut crème reduction is salted just enough to balance the intense sweetness of clarified Alphonso mangoes.

₹320
12-Hour Slow-Cooked Signature

Slow Cooked Lamb

Hand-Pulled Lamb Saffron Basmati Cardamom Consommé

A signature culinary highlight. Tender, spiced hand-pulled lamb slow-cooked for 12 hours in aromatic spices, pressed and layered over long-grain saffron-infused basmati rice. Served in a shallow pool of rich, savory cardamom-scented broth.

The Craft Behind the Dish

Slow braised at 85°C to break down connective tissues for ultra-tender pulled texture, then pan-seared to lock in moisture and natural juices before layering.

₹480
